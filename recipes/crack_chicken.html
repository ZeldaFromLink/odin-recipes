Crack Chicken

Description

Crack Chicken is a delicious and healthy meal that can be prepared in a crock pot or similar slow cooker. This meal is fairly easy to make and can be seasoned however you like. It is keto friendly, but most importantly, delicious!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Ranch dressing seasoning mix
  • Cream cheese
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Cooked bacon
  • Green onions(optional)

Steps

  1. Add chicken thighs to slow cooker.
  2. Add ranch seasoning and cream cheese.
  3. Cook on low for 7 hours (do not open lid during this time).
  4. After the chicken is cooked, shred with two forks (or a method of your choosing).
  5. Now you can add your cooked bacon, cheese, and green onions as desired.
